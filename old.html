Profile

Hello! Welcome to my site! I started out in Taiwan and came to the US when I was 15. After completing highschool I went to University of Illinois to study Electrical and Computer Engineering. After college I did an electrical engineering job for a bit and took an interest in web development. After a lot of messing around I finally built my first web app: Baconbnb. It is a clone of the popular site Airbnb back in 2013. Currently I am a front end developer at Chegg Inc. and I love it! The front end is a lot of fun. I enjoy building amazing UIs and thinking about user engagement. Needless to say there is still a lot to learn and I keep myself busy by building apps with current technologies.
